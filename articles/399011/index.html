On November 8, in Digital October, the blockchain school will host a lecture crowdfunding on the blockchain.

On November 8, in the building of the Moscow Digital October center, the final occupation of the Blockchain School's educational project will take place - a series of master classes in various blockchain systems with practical case studies.

The lecturer will be the founder and CEO of the blockchain platform Waves, Alexander Ivanov. The lesson will detail the pros and cons of VC & Crowdfunding financing. On the example of the success of Waves, the model of proper ICO organization will be revealed. Alexander will make a comparison and review of the blockchain systems, after which he will answer all questions.

The meeting will take place in the Digital October center at the following address: Moscow, Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6, bldg. 3, 4th floor. Beginning at 18:30. Free registration - by reference .

The first lesson of the educational program of this year was held on October 18. At the previous meetings in the theoretical part, the guests listened to a brief introduction to Bitcoin and the blockchain, understood the Bitcoin blockchain technology, EmerCoin, Steemit and Voice blockchain platform, platforms for creating decentralized online services based on the Ethereum blockchain. In the practical part, they compared different blockchain systems, analyzed case studies of joint-stock company and shares based on it using Kolionovo as an example, tried to create a private test network (--dev --networkid 1101), developed a prototype of the exchanger of different cryptocurrencies and solved a lot of other interesting problems.
