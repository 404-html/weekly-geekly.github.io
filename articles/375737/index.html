Muscovites will receive SMS about the air quality in the capital



Now the Moscow authorities are developing a service that will allow residents of the capital to receive SMS about the state of the atmosphere in the city. Messages will not come to everyone, but on request (perhaps the developers will make available and subscribe to the distribution of messages in automatic mode). To receive an SMS, you will need to send an outgoing message to number 7377 (registered with the mayor's office). Some time after sending such a message, the owner of the phone will receive an incoming SMS with air quality data.

Work on the project is carried out jointly with the Moscow Department of Information Technology (DIT). Now Muscovites (and not only they) can find exhaustive information about the quality of air in Moscow on the website of Moscow Monitoring . And the introduction of the sms-service is planned to expand the range of informed residents of the city.

By the way, in Moscow with the Internet services for the residents of the capital, the situation is very good. So, with the help of SMS, you can check the availability and size of traffic police fines, find out the debt on utility bills and even transfer meter readings to water in the housing and utilities sector.

More recently, several air pollution cases have been reported in Moscow at once. The most notable such incident occurred in early November, when Muscovites could smell hydrogen sulphide, petroleum products and sulfur.
