Sony has launched a crowdfunding platform for its own employees

Sony launched the First Flight crowdfunding platform to promote the business ideas of its own employees. The platform will allow you to pre-order devices created by Sony developers.

Sony CEO Kazuo Hirai encourages employees to create new gadgets and technology, while some units do not show the best financial results. In February 2015, she singled out from the corporation into separate business units departments dealing with audio and video technologies. A year earlier, the Vaio notebook division and the development of TV sets separated from the company.

The company plans to leave three main areas:
  • Sony Pictures Entertainment - a Hollywood studio ;
  • PlayStation is one of the most profitable parts of Sony thanks to PS4 sales;
  • Image Sensors - the unit is successful largely due to the use of products in the Apple iPhone and owns 40 percent of the world market CMOS-matrices .
