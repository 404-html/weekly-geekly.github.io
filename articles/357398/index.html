The attackers hacked the network of HBO channel, taking away the script "Game of thrones"


Source: yahoo

The series "Game of Thrones" - one of the most popular in the history of television, including Internet TV. The plot is watched by hundreds of millions of viewers. It is clear that the creators of the series tried to make each episode released on the screen unexpectedly, so that the audience languished in anticipation of the next episode.

Accordingly, the company has to ensure that there is no leakage and information about upcoming Games of Thrones events has not entered the Internet. After all, each such leak is a decrease in the number of viewers, and hence a decrease in potential profits. For the time being, everything went well, with the exception of some trifles, but now HBO can be sounded alarmed - some hackers got to the servers, taking the script of the games of thrones that had not yet been released, as well as scripts and episodes of other TV series.

The company has already confirmed what happened, although it did not disclose the details of the hacking.
