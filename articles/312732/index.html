Micro DC. What is it?

Introduction


Since the cloud theme is now very fashionable, the relevance of the data center is also great. But if you have an average enterprise size and there is no separate data center as superfluous, then vendors can offer you


  1. Modular data center.
  2. Mini data center. (miniDC).

Actually the last and stop. The name is very beautiful. But in fact, maybe you already have it. If not, you can do it.



What is it?


The proposed solutions in reality are a 19 "server cabinet mounted inside:


  1. Server (or several).
  2. Network devices (switches and routers)
  3. UPS.
  4. Organizers, patch panels, fans, power panels.
  5. Monitoring system.
  6. Service contract.

Everything except paragraph 6 is completely obvious. Under the service contract, everything becomes clear if we take into account two circumstances. Warranty periods for all components are different, with the purchase of a contract, the guarantee is given for everything for the duration of the contract. That is, with a 3-year contract, the fact that UPSs in practice are guaranteed for 2 years from the moments of release, and fans for 12 months, does not matter. The second important aspect is that no manufacturer manufactures all the components on its own, except for its ODM assembly products and with the guarantee of its internal squabbles.


From the advantages of this approach. On the site you choose a set of "giblets" and warranty conditions. Get ready to connect the cabinet assembly. The downside is obvious. Price. Self-collection will be cheaper.
