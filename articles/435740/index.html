Registration for GraphQL Meetup in St. Petersburg is open



We invite you to Piter GraphQL Meetup , which will be held on January 24 at the St. Petersburg office of Wrike. We are waiting for developers of any level: both those already using GraphQL, and those who are looking at a promising technology.

In a programme:

1. Dmitry Mamonov, Wrike backend architect

Topic: GraphQL and Web Application Architecture

What is common between GraphQL and SQL, how GraphQL changes the architecture of Web applications, what are the fundamental problems in current implementations of the GraphQL concept itself.

2. Pavel Chertorogov , developer of Internet products, open-source maintainer . Since the end of 2015, focuses on isomorphic applications. In the summer of 2016, he began the development of graphql-compose in the open source.

Topic: Design and design patterns GraphQL-schemes

3. Alexey Zolotykh, frontend developer at Wrike. Engaged in infrastructure, research and talks about it at conferences and meetings.

Topic: GraphQL - New Redux

GraphQL is another standard for server requests. So many people think. In his report, Alexey will try to prove that GraphQL is also a qualitative leap in the development of the frontend as a whole. And the world will never be the same.

We are waiting for you at the Wrike office on January 24th at 6.30pm .
