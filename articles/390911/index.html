Geektimes + MWC2016 and all - all - all



Hi, Geektimes!

Surely, you have already noticed how much is around the news from Mobile World Congress 2016, where companies one by one seek to be the first to showcase their latest products and gadgets.

This year, Geektimes has his own correspondent on # MWC16 - Kpyto, thanks ilya42 for assistance in obtaining accreditation and thanks to icover for flexible working hours )


Even before the exhibition, I left a small comment under the article about the output of the Caterpillar smartphone with a thermal imager, where Geektimes readers asked their additional questions about the device presented at the MWC.

I invite everyone to leave their questions, requests and suggestions under this publication - I will try to find time and opportunity to answer the maximum number of questions (by redirecting them to representatives on the stands).

I can not promise that any wish will be fulfilled, but if you have long wanted to ask something "edakoe" from representatives of tech giants or from other companies that are represented on the MWC16 - this is your chance.

At the moment, I attended presentations and stands of ZTE, Huawei, Samsung and several small, but very interesting companies, saw interesting wearable gadgets, smart glasses and, striking me personally, an exhibition section dedicated to companies working with graphene. Materials about this expect on Geektimes soon.

Thanks for attention! Before contact, and do not pull with questions - MWC will last another three days.
