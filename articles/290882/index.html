The foundation, founded by RVC and R-Pharm, has invested 160 million rubles in software for analyzing data from medical examinations.

The Russian fund RBV Capital invested 160 million rubles in Image Analysis Ltd. The company develops software for visualization of magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) examinations in the treatment of immune and oncological diseases.

The funds raised will be used to further develop the Dynamic software platform, to develop its clinical centers and increase the client base of Image Analysis.

Today, the market for software for quantitative analysis of data from medical examinations is actively developing. The software is used to diagnose inflammatory and oncological diseases, diseases of the musculoskeletal system, neurodegenerative diseases, cardiac perfusion and a number of other disorders.

Image Analysis is the leader among similar developments. Previously, doctors had to get the results of the survey, relying only on the qualitative assessment, their own experience and the patient's reaction.

The development of Image Analysis allows specialists to carry out a digital analysis of the dynamics of the disease and identify intermediate results of treatment. The use of such software improves diagnostic accuracy and at the same time reduces its cost. In addition, the process of diagnostics itself is significantly accelerated, the company said in a press release.

Olga Kubassova, Ph.D., Founder and CEO Image Analysis Ltd .:
We are glad to see a growing number of clinical and research companies using Dynamic when performing MRI. We believe that in the conditions of Russia with its vast territory and the shortage of qualified personnel, especially in the regions, our product will be extremely in demand. A significant contribution to the development of the company's current product was made by Russian and Belarusian specialists, and we intend to continue this cooperation.

Andrey Kozlov, Investment Director, RBV Capital, Member of the Board of Directors of Image Analysis Ltd .:
I am glad to have the opportunity to work with the professional team of Image Analysis Ltd. In modern medicine, there is a growing demand for quantifying the results of MRI examinations based on detailed data analysis and objective standards. This opportunity provides a unique development of Image Analysis. We are pleased to support the development of the company, as well as help it with the development of a promising Russian market.

The founders of the fund are RVC and the company R-Farm . RBV Capital was founded in 2014. The fund intends to invest half of its funds in Russian companies.
