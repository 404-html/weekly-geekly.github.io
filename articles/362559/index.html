MB Chronowing on video: smart watches that look like classic

Having officially announced MB Chronowing last week, Michael Bastian and Hewlett-Packard showed only images of their simultaneously smart and interesting hours. Now it is possible to evaluate the novelty in the video (although, basically it‚Äôs a render).



The video shows how the implementation of various functions of the watch looks like. Among them, besides the usual notifications about unread messages in social networks and the weather, there is also a tracking of sports scores. Michael Bastian also talks about the main features: full support for devices running Android and iOS, 7 days of battery life, water resistance and 3 straps included.



Despite the lack of a color display and control solely through buttons, many online publications have noted that the watch is interesting due to the fusion of classic design with the capabilities of a smart watch. In its type, MB Chronowing resembles primarily Pebble Steel .

Two variations of watches will be on sale: a regular ($ 349) and a limited ($ 649) version, the difference of which lies in the sapphire crystal and the alligator strap. The first buyers will receive MB Chronowing on November 7th.
