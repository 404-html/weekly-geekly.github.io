How much are Darth Vader armor



The black suit of Darth Vader complements perhaps the most famous image of the villain in science fiction. This is a necessary accessory - a portable life support system compensates for the serious injuries that Anakin Skywalker received in a duel with Obi-Wan Kenobi. According to the Star Wars encyclopedia , the costume is made in the ancient traditions of the Sith, according to which the warriors of the dark side of the Force must decorate themselves with heavy armor. At the same time, it is a high-tech and rather expensive device.
