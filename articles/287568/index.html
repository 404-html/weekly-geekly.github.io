Innovations in analyzing online media traffic sources

Did you know that there is a tool on the Internet that allows you to analyze which audience directs each individual traffic source to the Internet media site? This tool is a detailed report on the sources of visitors and is implemented in the form of filters.


With the help of filters, you can determine what the visitors who come from a particular source are interested in and what they do on the media site, which headings they read, which messages are most popular among them, how much time they spend on the site and how many pages they browse, and you can also see the regional distribution (countries and cities) of visitors from this source.

Such a characterization of traffic from a specific source allows Internet media to more adequately evaluate traffic partners and adjust cooperation with them so that in the end get the audience most interesting to advertisers and consistent with the goals of the publication. The advertisers themselves, characterizing the interests and behavior of visitors to the media site, will help to more effectively distribute the budget in a crisis.

To receive such data about your Internet-media - just register it on the portal "Inforotor" (it is written about registration here )
