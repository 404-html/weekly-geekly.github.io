What's new in Android 9 Pie for Sony Xperia smartphones

Sony Mobile continues to distribute the operating system Android 9 Pie for flagship smartphones, as well as clarified data on the availability of updates for devices of the middle price segment.



For the first time, the company shared plans for updating smartphones in mid-August and named approximate dates for the release of firmware. Devices receive updates in groups, for example, the first smartphones with updates over the air have already become Xperia XZ2 and XZ2 Compact. Depending on the region, the owners of these devices have been able to download the new firmware since October 12. The second wave of the firmware distribution included Xperia XZ Premium, XZ1 and XZ1 Compact smartphones, the update started on November 10th. Android 9 Pie for Xperia XZ2 Premium is expected to be released soon.

The company also disclosed information about the release of Android 9 for the mid-priced Xperia XA2, XA2 Ultra and XA2 Plus models and other smartphones will begin to receive an update from March 4, 2019. Previously it was only known that the update is scheduled for the first quarter of next year. As for the Sony Xperia XZ3, the flagship of the second half of 2018, this smartphone works on the latest version of the operating system out of the box.



New software for Sony smartphones includes Android security update from October 1, 2018, so that the device will receive the current level of user data protection.
