The world's first football robots managed via Twitter

Innopolis University together with TRIK Company and N + 1 website launched a football match between two robots - red and green. Each of them can be controlled via Twitter using special commands. The broadcast is available at: www.hitbox.tv/innopolisu . The match lasts from 14:00 to 21:00 Moscow time.



To send a command on Twitter, write with spaces the tag # wed2016, the color of the robot and the action.

Names of robots:
red or red green (green) or green

Commands in Russian:
forward
backwards
to the left
to the right
turn

Commands in English:
forward
backward
left
right
turn

Case and punctuation marks are not counted. Examples:
# VRO2016 red left
green forward! WRO2016
# VRO2016 reversal, red
green go the right # VRO2016

Every 5 seconds, the robot analyzes the commands on Twitter and executes the most popular command. The particular piquancy of the battle adds the possible delay of the server for online broadcasts.

The video broadcast comes from the audience of Innopolis University, where the final stage of the All-Russian Robotic Olympiad is currently taking place.
