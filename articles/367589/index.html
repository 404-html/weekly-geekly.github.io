Sony Xperia Z5 is available in pink color

The central model of the line of smartphones Sony Xperia Z5 added a new color scheme. The flagship in ash-pink color complements the existing set of their emerald green, white, black and gold colors. The choice of the new color according to the designers of Sony harmoniously fits into any style and expresses the power and spirit of the times.



Specifications of the device have not changed: it is still equipped with a 5.2-inch Full HD display, 64-bit eight-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 processor, 3 GB of RAM, 32 GB of internal memory. It also has a microSD slot with support for cards up to 200 GB, a fingerprint scanner, a 23 megapixel built-in camera and a 2,900 mAh battery. The new Xperia Z5 Pink will be available in versions with one and two SIM cards.





Pink Sony Xperia Z5 will go on sale in February at a price of 44,990 rubles.
