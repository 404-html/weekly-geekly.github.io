Strategic partnership between Dental Cloud and KPI Lab

Dental Cloud and KPI Lab have announced a strategic partnership. As part of the partnership, the companies have joined forces to create a project office whose goal will be to implement large-scale automation projects in large branch and state dental clinics.




The role of Dental Cloud in partnership is to transfer its automation platform to projects, and KPI Lab to provide its competencies in creating Dashbord for managers of medium and large companies and automating company processes.
