All space research missions on one infographic

Almost sixty years have passed since the launch of the first artificial satellite of the Earth. Since that time, spacecraft, animals and people have been sent to space repeatedly. Studio Pop Chart Lab has compiled an infographic in which all probes sent to space for the study of other planets and stars were possibly mentioned.



The image below is clickable. At the top of the poster you can see the path that the spacecraft overcame, and at the bottom - how these devices looked. In the table, the probes are divided in directions: to Venus - you can see the Soviet "Venus" , to the Moon - Luna 16, 20 and 24, to Mars - Spirit, Opportunity , Curiousity , to Pluto - New Horizons .

A 39x27 inch poster is available for pre-order . A great gift to a person who is interested in space.
