Florida residents were fined $ 48 thousand for using the powerful jammer of cellular networks on the road

The gadget was turned on every time Jason Humphreys went to the city of Tampa, and he rode there every day.

Commission agents and the police tried to locate the source of interference on the highway number 4 for a long time. And only a few days later the owner of the jammer was found and arrested.
