Android 7.0 Nougat will be available on Sony smartphones in December

Good news! Sony smartphones are about to get an upgrade to Android 7.0 (Nougat). The first devices to support the latest operating system will be Xperia XZ and Xperia X Performance .



Owners of top models Xperia will be able to upgrade to the end of December. A little later, the update will be available on Sony Xperia X and X Compact, XA and XA Ultra smartphones, as well as Xperia Z3 +, Z5, Z5 Compact, Z5 Premium and on the Xperia Z4 Tablet.

In addition to better optimization and more impressive indicators of autonomy, Android 7.0 will add a multi-coconate mode in which you can simultaneously open any two applications and quickly switch between them. In addition, the Xperia X Performance will appear completely manual shooting mode for the main camera, with the possibility of manual focus and shutter speed control. Well, the Xperia menu will get full integration with the Google Now service.
