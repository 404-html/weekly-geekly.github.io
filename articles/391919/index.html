ISP in Moscow offers lifetime free internet

Finally, the time has come when the Internet has become free. True, so far only in Moscow. The unusual campaign ‚Äú Infinite Internet ‚Äù was launched by the NetByNet provider (a subsidiary of the operator ‚ÄúMegafon‚Äù).

The conditions are simple. When buying a Wi-Fi router Wifire S1010 for 3990 rubles. The client receives a lifetime free Internet access at a speed of 5 Mbit / s. If you do not watch online video and do not play games, then this is quite enough for many users: children, elderly people who often leave for business trips, or just as a spare free channel for emergency access to the Network in the event of a failure on the main channel.

Wifire S1010 is a good router with 4 internal antennas, built-in USB, and support 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies.

The promotion is valid until the end of May 2016, but if successful, it can be extended, and it can also be held in all regions of Russia where the operator works.

In this way, the provider intends to increase the user base. They are going to earn money not only from the sale of routers, but also from the service of a temporary increase in speed. The user can temporarily increase the speed up to 100 Mbps at any time.



NetByNet Mass Media Business Development Director Alexei Nazarov believes that most subscribers will most likely want to increase speed.

If you compare the cost of the tariff for unlimited Internet 5 Mbit / s with the tariffs of other providers, then the router for 3990 rubles. pays off in a couple of years. The main thing is that the provider itself does not go bankrupt before this time.

It seems that the NetByNet promotion is the first case in Russia when the provider offers free broadband Internet, albeit at a low speed. In the beginning of March, VimpelCom launched a similar campaign - users can connect to broadband Internet and pay TV in just 1 rub. per month, provided that they are VimpelCom subscribers at any package rate, except for the cheapest one.
