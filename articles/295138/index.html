Hola, amigos. Yandex plans expansion into Latin American markets


Photo: Eraldo Peres / AP

The company Yandex now works in the markets of Russia, Ukraine, Turkey and some other CIS countries. Nevertheless, the company management plans to expand, diversify the business, and now plans to enter the markets of Latin America. Soon the first application will be launched, intended only for the countries of this region. The application itself is called Yandex Launcher, and it is intended for the Android OS, they write "Vedomosti". The program is designed for audiences in Brazil and Mexico - here, Yandex has already distributed its products. At the same time, Brazil, Mexico and Colombia are the most actively growing markets in Latin America.

Yandex Launcher is a shell for Android gadgets that allows users to customize the mobile operating system for themselves. At the same time, the program can run on budget smartphones with not the highest performance. Interestingly, Yandex Launcher is not available to Russian users, and there are no company services in this application. For example, Google is offered here as a search service, but you can also choose Bing - these search engines work in Latin American countries.
