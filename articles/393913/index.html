The release of DOOM



ID Software invented a time machine and returned to me in 1993. Thank! (C) From Steam


Tonight, without fanfare and dust, Bethesda studio and id Software have released a new part of the legendary shooter DOOM (working title: Doom 4).

Reviews on Steam speak for themselves:



As expected, id Software did not disappoint expectations and arranged a vigorous restart of the legendary series, crossing the setting of the classic DOOM with the dynamics of Quake III Arena fights. A degree of violence that goes beyond the scale, the finishing of enemies and the gloomy setting will not leave DOOM fans from the 90s indifferent:


Finishing off the enemy

In short, no PG-13, only R, blood and hardcore.

System requirements cannot be called democratic, but this is not the worst that we have seen in recent years:



You can purchase and play the new DOOM on your PC using Steam . Also new shooter is available for PS4 and Xbox One. For those who want to get a "physical copy" you can purchase DOOM on the disk through the official website of the game . There is also a collector's edition for sale with a 30-centimeter figurine of a revenant and a metal, with its own design, box for a disk. Issue price for the collector's edition - 9999 RUB.
