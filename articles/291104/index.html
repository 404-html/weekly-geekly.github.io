FRIA will help Rostec to find and develop new IT projects

The Internet Initiatives Development Foundation (IIDF) and the state corporation Rostec have signed a memorandum of partnership at the conference ‚ÄúInformation Technologies in the Security Service of the Military-Industrial Complex‚Äù. IIDF will look for projects that meet the key tasks of Rostec.

Rostec will inform FRIA of the demand for projects and will consider the possible introduction of ready-made technologies and projects into the state corporation structures.
