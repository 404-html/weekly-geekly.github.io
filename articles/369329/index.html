Tesla Model Xmas: electric cars can entertain



Now there are not so many owners of the new Tesla Model X electric vehicles in the world. This new SUV is good for everyone except, perhaps, prices. The average cost of such an electric vehicle is more than $ 100 thousand .

Before Christmas, Tesla decided to arrange a show with additional features of their cars. In particular, systems of several electric cars simultaneously reproduced the same melody, flashed headlights, opened and closed the doors. Perhaps this will serve as an additional incentive for buyers who are still not too good at parting with their money in exchange for electric cars and off-road vehicles.

Model Xmas show! This will be done on your own car. https://t.co/sZGq8XeeTn

- Elon Musk (@elonmusk) December 25, 2015


All Model X will be supplied with the ability to arrange the show to their owners, said Ilon Musk.

So far, however, it is not entirely clear whether it is possible to use some other melody, or a show can be arranged only with the music that sounds in the video ( Wizards in Winter by Trans-Siberian Orchestra ).
