WhatsApp Overcame 800 Million Active Members

Today, WhatsApp has surpassed the 800 million monthly active users per month, so now many are asking themselves if the most expensive Facebook acquisition in the near future can show a figure of 1 billion for this indicator.

On Friday evening, Jan Kum, the CEO and co-founder of WhatsApp, shared this news with the public on a personal page on Facebook.

We remind you: last year Facebook bought WhatsApp for $ 19 billion, and the acquisition gained another 100 million active monthly users every 4 months since August, when WhatsApp used about 600 million people a month. In January 2015, this figure was already equal to 700 million.

WhatsApp remains the leader in active audience among mobile applications. In February, Twitter reported about 288 million active users per month; last December, Instagram reported about 300 million active monthly users. In December 2014, there were 1.39 million active users on Facebook itself.
