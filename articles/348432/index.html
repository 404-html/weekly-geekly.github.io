QIWI invites designers, researchers and analysts to the kitchen

On February 15, 2018, a QIWI kitchen will take place at the QIWI office. The kitchen is dedicated to creating products from scratch. The speakers will be the QIWI design teams, as well as Ivan Zamesin (ex-Chatfuel) and Alexey Chernyak (ex-Groupon).



The new project QIWI Kitchen is a series of informal meetings for designers, researchers and analysts who want to quickly launch new products and see the QIWI's internal "kitchen" and how everything is arranged in large companies in fact.
Participants of the first meeting will be able to learn about the processes when creating products from scratch, why the whole team needs to know the business model, how to quickly and easily make customer development.
