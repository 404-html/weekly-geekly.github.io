Zenbo - friendly home robot

On the eve of the exhibition Computex 2016 (will be held from May 31 to June 4), ASUS presented its new developments. Including before the public appeared and the home robot Zenbo.

Introduced as a family-friendly and friendly home-based robot, Zenbo is able to independently navigate the house, recognize faces, search the Internet, support voice control, take pictures, and do a lot of interesting and useful things.



According to the head of ASUSTeK Computer, Johnny Shih:
For decades, people have dreamed of having such a domestic companion: smart, sweet, and always willing to serve. Our goal is to bring robotic technology to every home.



The most intriguing aspect of Zenbo's capabilities is its ability to connect to different devices. For example, a robot can join a TV, an air conditioner, a washing machine and other equipment. Zenbo is able to independently monitor dangerous situations - for example, the fall of a person - and notify his family and friends of a similar incident. By receiving such news, a person can take control of the robot and assess the situation using the built-in camera. In addition, Zenbo is able to turn off any of the household appliances to prevent a possible fire or flood.



Over time, ASUS plans to expand the functionality of its creation. If you believe the manufacturer, Zenbo learns to remind of events in the calendar, like a planned trip to the cinema, to a doctor, hairdresser, etc.

For kids, Zenbo serves as a gaming partner, learning and entertainment device. He can organize an exciting program with dancing to music or reading books from the built-in digital library.



The company is also promoting a Zenbo software development project with access to a software development kit for creating new applications.

A friendly Zenbo will cost $ 599. But the exact release date of the robot is still unknown.
