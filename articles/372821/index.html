Professional social network Viadeo liquidated its Russian office


Viadeo is a professional social network that operates on the same principle as the leader of the segment, the social network LinkedIn. Under the law on personal data, the company had to deploy servers to store personal data of Russians in the territory of the Russian Federation, however, like its direct competitor LinkedIn, it did not. In total, Viadeo has at its disposal data on 1.5 million Russians, which are stored on servers in the United States.

Viadeo liquidates its office in Russia because of the unprofitability of a Russian subsidiary, as reported by the newspaper Kommersant . In this case, the closure of the office does not mean the closure of the Russian version of the site. The address ru.viadeo.com still works fully.

Judging the closure of the office can be, first of all, by the state of the Russian legal entity LLC Viadeo Independent Media, which, according to the website kartoteka.ru, is in the process of liquidation.
