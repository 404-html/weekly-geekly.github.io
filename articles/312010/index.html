Google Online Conference: Progressive Web Apps Day (October 11)

Welcome We invite everyone to join the Google online conference on Progressive Web Apps on October 11th at 11:00 (Moscow time).



Our progressive speakers



Sergey Pugachev, Google


Andrey Lipatsev, Google

Maxim Salnikov , AngularJS GDE

Zviad Kardava , IoT GDE

Program


11:00 - 11:30 Introduction to Progressive Web Apps, Sergey Pugachev
11:40 - 12:30 Immersion in Service Workers, Maxim Salnikov
12:40 - 13:10 Improving user experience: installation and push notifications, Sergey Pugachev
13:20 - 13:50 PWA implementation with the help of the Angular Mobile Toolkit, Maxim Salnikov
14:00 - 14:30 Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP): speeding up the loading of web pages, Andrey Lipattsev
14:40 - 15:10 Interaction with Bluetooth devices from Web applications, Zviad Kardava

See you at Progressive Web Apps Day!

Ps. Of course, further conference recordings will be available for viewing, but it is much more interesting to get the latest news directly on the online conference!
