In three years, cumulative online sales in the US, China, Germany and the UK will reach $ 1 trillion

According to a joint study by the Dutch consulting company OC & C Strategy Consultants, Google and PayPal, the total amount of online sales in the USA, China, Germany and the UK will increase by 100% and reach a trillion dollars. To date, this amount is $ 490 billion.

Against the background of a difficult situation in the global economy, the international online retail market continues to grow. For example, when buying British goods online, the Chinese spend 2.7 times, and the Germans - 1.7 times more than the British themselves. The frequency of visits to British online stores, the Chinese are not inferior to residents of the UK.

The growth of the e-commerce market is due to the high demand for electronics, the continued spread of the Internet among the population and the introduction of Internet technologies in the business model.
