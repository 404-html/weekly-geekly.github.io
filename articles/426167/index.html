Story: An unusual trip Kimi.


Kimi Tornado rides to work. She is driven by an autonomous electric car provided by the plant for the production of exoskeletons and robotic elements to create new artificial medical prostheses. Kimi works at this plant as a senior engineer for the installation and commissioning of equipment by customers. Today she has a business trip to another country and to a faraway city, in which new electronic prostheses for a little girl are very much awaited. These prostheses are in the trunk of an electric car, and in Kimi‚Äôs hands is a folder with documents on business trip and equipment. The folder also contains a ticket for the 14A flight of the ANA Flight # 008 of the new airliner called ‚ÄúThe Crystal Sky‚Äù and a photo of the little smiling girl Sofia, who is very much waiting for Kimi's arrival on 06.28.2017 to San Francisco.

At check-in and loading problems with containers from the baggage Kimi did not arise. Kimi passed the control and started to prepare her work schedule for the next week in the waiting room on the tablet computer. The bracelet-sensor on her arm showed the data from the self-diagnosis program, that the prostheses are OK, their systems are stable, the batteries are charged, and the parameters fit perfectly to the dimensions that they received from the medical center for Sofia.

Announced landing. Kimi took her seat 14C, listened to the voice of the captain of the ship, who spoke about the ANA Flight # 008 route from Tokyo to San Francisco, and then during take-off she closed her eyes and slowly fell asleep to the lulling sounds of engines. She prepared and checked the prosthetic configuration all night. In the porthole, to the left of Kimi, black storm clouds appeared somewhere far away. Flight ANA Flight # 008 should have passed them tangentially. And the captain almost did it. The same lightning winding touched the bottom of the hull of flight # 008 in the luggage compartment area, where Kimi's containers were located. The bracelet sensor on Kimi‚Äôs hand started, flushed, and then evaporated altogether. Kimi also evaporated with the bracelet ...

- What would you prefer for lunch today? - Kimi heard a soft but unusual voice.
Kimi opened her eyes, stood in front of her, or rather even positioned herself, a strange unit very similar to a man, but it was a robot with its head, arms and torso!

‚ÄúWell, I'll come to you later,‚Äù the robot steward said even softer and rolled the trolley further along the aisle of the plane.

- Attention, weighting and tactile tuning is performed! - Kimi heard already a female voice from somewhere below below.

The chair, in which Kimi sat, began to change. It widened slightly, deepened in the back area and went up by a dozen centimeters. On the armrests, where Kimi's hands were lying, small pads, which touched Kimi's wrists, moved forward. On the monitor on the front of the back of another chair, a picture appeared with the parameters of pulse, pressure, heartbeat and body temperature Kimi. The readings were a bit overstated, but not critical. Kimi felt a fresh breeze from above. This system of ventilation of the 14C began to supply additional portions of oxygen to the comfort zone of its passenger.

‚ÄúI wonder if this is a dream or something unusual is happening here?‚Äù Thought Kimi, but decided to calm down a bit so as not to attract additional attention of the 14C robot armchair.

Kimi gently touched the screen with her hand in front of her. Choosing to "find out the weather," Kimi gasped softly. The screen showed weather information at the point of arrival (correct, according to this Kimi ticket). But the date of arrival was 06.28.2037. At this moment, Kimi raised her hands to her face, trying to close the escaping scream, but found that she did not have a wristband on her arm. Instead, just a small strip of slightly darkened skin around the wrist.

‚ÄúLet's say it happened (something with time and space), try to understand why this happened and how I will continue to be,‚Äù Kimi thought. The engineer is always an engineer, even in fantastic situations. She tried to rise from the chair and look around. But it was not easy, because the chair seemed to envelop Kimi. Thus, Kimi could move relatively freely, but she could not get up. Standing up and resting her arms on the armrests, she saw a very spacious and bright cabin. ‚ÄúI ask you to choose,‚Äù said the robot-steward and handed Kimi the tablet menu and stood waiting.

In the event that the passenger is outside the seat in the cabin, then the robot-steward is responsible for his safety. Also, a robot steward uses a special food replicator to organize the delivery and delivery of drinks and food to passengers.

‚ÄúThe sky is our new Earth,‚Äù the lecture with pictures on the screen continued on the history of ANA.

- robot-steward (first implementation 08/08/2019) - a robotic humanoid android with artificial intelligence that can replace on board a steward, mechanic, cleaner, technicians;

- Robo-seat (the first introduction of 02.03.2020) - is responsible for the safety of passengers during landing and descent, is also an individual rescue capsule for the passenger during the entire flight;

- the system of food replicators (first implementation 03.01.2021) - is responsible for providing passengers with almost any form of food that can be imagined, any dish is generated using food printers and issued to the passenger on his order;

The same take-off of the aircraft takes place with the help of starting magnetic acceleration. Now not only aircraft began to be equipped with smart systems, but also the airports themselves;

- modularity of all aircraft structures (first implementation 04/08/2035) - now it was possible to prepare and assemble an airplane of any configuration in one hour in a robotic hangar at the airport - wings with solar panels, engines and a modular building for a different number of seats, at the end of the assembly Robo chairs are installed in the plane, which already know the parameters of their passengers from the registration data and prepare their configuration for them. That is why the robo chair under Kimi began to change - there was no primary data about the passenger.

- There is no information about your data, only guest mode is available or enter your credentials separately, please, - a voice from the sphere sounded.

‚ÄúOf course not, where can they come from here,‚Äù thought Kimi.

But how the sphere could get the data from Kimi's finger and check them on its base was incomprehensible. Maybe this interface also scanned the face and retina remotely. It was interesting and Kimi really wanted to know the details.

‚ÄúActivate guest mode,‚Äù Kimi asked.

A green surface appeared on the sphere with the words "guest mode, use hand control".

Kimi knew and knew how this works, but she came across this interface for the first time. Hands could twist the sphere. It could be expanded simply by taking and pushing its boundaries. Inside the sphere were different programs - Internet browsers, gaming and television applications. Kimi really wanted to go to the news department and read information about the current day and what happened in the last twenty years in the world. - At the moment the room is free, you can visit it, please go to the tail end of the plane along track number two or into the front of the plane along track number one, the passage pattern on the screen, - said the robo-chair and started to push Kimy out of it a bit lifting the back of the chair and the lower part so that Kimi could easily get up and go where necessary.

‚ÄúThat's why I couldn't get up earlier,‚Äù Kimi thought.

Kimi climbed out of the robo chair, the side of the chair 14C turned green. It is very convenient not to look for the number of your seat upon return, but simply go to the green color.
After stretching her legs and arms, Kimi decided to go first to the front of the plane along the first long lane. Along the sides and in the middle of the plane, there were four rows of robo chairs, between them there were three lanes. A part of the robo-chairs was lit in the side in blue, the passengers were sleeping there, some were glowing in yellow, there were open panoramic windows, only Kimi's robo-chair and another chair in the tail of the plane glowed green.

The cabin was quiet. As if the plane was floating above the ground, and not rushing at a tremendous speed at high altitude. The sound of the engines was almost indistinguishable.

Kimi walked a bit forward along the aisle, moms sat in some robots with the children, some passengers worked with the virtual sphere, watched entertainment programs or communicated using video applications.

- Something to help you? He asked very quietly.

‚ÄúThen go to the end of the cabin to the sector with simulators, there are any to choose from, you can use them during the entire flight,‚Äù the robot-steward kindly suggested.

- Thank you, be sure to go there, - said Kimi.

She was still amazed at how many discoveries she had already made and what awaits her ahead.

On the way back, Kimi walked along a different path, passed another robot steward, who, near the robot chair with his mother and two children, tried to make a little clumsy figure toy out of oblong small balloons.

"It will be necessary to take into account that such servicing robots not only have to fulfill their direct duties, but also provide them with the opportunity to show additional opportunities for communication with passengers and self-study," Kimi noted.

At the end of the cabin there was a large sector for recreation and various simple sports training. The sanitary room was also not small, as Kimi was used to, but rather large and comfortable. Moreover, these rooms were immediately four in a row to minimize the queues of passengers.

And all the modules are also calculated from the passenger data and are completed before departure. Rest rooms and other comfort zones are also modularly installed according to the number of passengers.

It‚Äôs very convenient if you bought tickets for the flight and registered forty passengers, and an hour before the departure ten more passengers decided to go on the same flight, then a plane with fifty-five robots, five robots-stewards, five lounges, etc. will be prepared in the service hangar. .d That is, for a certain number of passengers, one number of aircraft modules is assembled and then scaled when the flight is assembled.

And the limit on the maximum and minimum number of passengers is now becoming more blurred. This all makes it more flexible and convenient organization of the airline ANA.
