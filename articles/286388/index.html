Freelancers Look At Media wrote a collective letter and threatened to hold the court

Co-founder of Look At Media and The Village editor-in-chief Alexei Ametov published an open letter in his personal Facebook account confirming that Look At Me publishing house had an unhealthy situation with fees paid to freelance authors, photographers and illustrators.

And although Alexey Ametov promises that the debts will be closed and from the 1st of June all the employees will know the terms of the salary debts repayment, today the story has been continued. Until June 1, all editions of the publication also ceased relations with freelancers, in order to save money and stabilize relations.

In the face of Anton Berkasov, one of the freelancers Look At Media, freelancers from the publishing house addressed the open letter , whose wage arrears ranged from 2 to 8 months, and the amount of debt on wages ranged from several thousand to 150 thousand rubles.

Anton Berkasov also writes that if the Look At Media publishing house does not pay wage arrears until April 1 of this year, which appeared until March 3, 2015, the employees will go to court to recover the debt.
