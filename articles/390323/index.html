On Safe Internet Day, Google distributes 2 GB per security check



In honor of World Safe Internet Day on February 8, Google gives all users 2 GB of storage space on Google Disk simply for the user to perform a Security Check .

The same action was last year, so you can increase the disk space by 2 gigabytes annually only in this way.

The check is very simple and consists of five steps, which check the phone and backup email, a list of connected devices, access settings, application passwords and two-step authentication settings.



The whole procedure takes less than a minute. Hurry up, February 8 in the USA ends on the morning of February 9, Moscow time. Although the action may be extended by a week, as last year.

UPD. According to the specified information, the action is valid until February 11 .
