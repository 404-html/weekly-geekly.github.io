Ministry of Communications: for the promotion of circumventing blocking sites will be responsible operators

For the time being, ordinary websites and users are not threatened with a story about how to access a website blocked in the Russian Federation


Photo: Ruslan Shamukov / TASS

Telecom operators should be responsible for promoting propaganda around website blocking, the Ministry of Communications believes.
