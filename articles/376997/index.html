Lenovo pocket projector displays a picture up to 110 inches in diagonal with a resolution of 854x480 pixels

Lenovo's pocket projector makes a screen with a diagonal of up to 110 inches out of any wall, works for two hours on one charge and connects to a smartphone via a DLNA wireless interface. The novelty supports microSD cards up to 32 gigabytes.

The projector requires a small area for placement, unlike a tablet with a Lenovo Yoga Tablet 2 Pro projector - my 13.3-inch beast occupies half a table.



Lenovo Pocket Projector is equipped with a LED light source with a brightness of 50 lumens and a lifetime of up to 20 thousand hours. Battery device allows you to watch movies and presentations up to two and a half hours. Unfortunately, the resolution is only 854x480 pixels.

Communication with a smartphone or tablet is carried out via a microUSB cable or a DLNA or Miracast wireless interface. Smartphone Requirements: Android 4.3 and up.

The size of the device is 104x25x100 millimeters. It consists of two parts: one stands on the plane, and the other can be rotated to adjust the angle of the image.

The cost of the gadget in the United States will be $ 250.
