Seven free automated task platforms for programming skills

Hello! Recently made a selection of free services for learning programming from scratch . In the comments received a lot of interest to us platforms. Of these, made a separate list of those that are suitable for professional programmers.

leetcode


Collection of programming tasks in different languages. The purpose of the service is to prepare programmers for the tasks that are found in the interview. The platform immediately gives feedback to the accuracy and effectiveness of the solution, shows solutions and allows you to discuss them with other participants. In the paid version, you can pass an automated interview in Google, Facebook or Amazon: the robot will pick up the questions, catch the time and even help to evaluate.
