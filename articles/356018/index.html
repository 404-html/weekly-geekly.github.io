The new bill obliges telecom operators to keep all subscriber data for 3 years

We are talking about voice data, text messages, images, sounds or other types of messages from Internet users and mobile subscribers.

The authors of the bill are two people - MP Spring and Senator Ozerov, writes "Roskomsvoboda".

The document itself introduces a number of significant changes in several laws. In particular, the authors of the document propose to add to the Law on Communication the following wording of clause 1 of Article 64: ‚ÄúTelecommunication operators are obliged to store information on the facts of reception, transmission, delivery and (or) processing of voice information in the territory of the Russian Federation for three years and text messages, including their content, as well as images, sounds or other messages of users of communication services ... ‚Äù.

Here there are two difficulties. The first is that the deputies are proposing to introduce monitoring of all users of Internet providers and cellular operators. The second is that in order to store such amount of data for three years, data warehouses with practically unlimited volume are needed.
