Medvedev: a balance must be maintained between the freedom of the Network and state regulation



A few days ago, Dmitry Medvedev, in an interview with the Chinese press, shared his thoughts on creating a code of conduct on the Internet that would act in the form of soft law. Speaking on December 15 to the participants of the Second World Conference on Internet Governance in the Chinese city of Wucheng, the Russian Prime Minister explained his position .

The main point is that the regulation of the Internet should be carried out at the international level, under the auspices of leading international institutions.
