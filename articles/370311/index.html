Uber resumed testing of its ro-mobiles three days after the accident



Service Uber resumed testing of unmanned vehicles of its own design after three days, after the accident, reports Engadget.

The accident occurred in the city of Tempe in Arizona. Robomobil collided with another car and rolled over. Only the driver was in the cabin, no one was hurt.

The police called the human factor a preliminary cause of the accident: another machine under the control of a living person did not miss the Uber's robility car, which led to a collision. It was reported that the testing of robotic mobiles will be suspended while the causes of the accident are being investigated.

However, Uber announced the resumption of tests until the end of the official investigation of the accident in Tempe.
