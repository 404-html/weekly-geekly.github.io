Moscow JS 40 in Avito - announcement

Hello! March 1 at the Avito office will be held Moscow JS 40 , an informal meeting of JavaScript-developers. Presentations will be made by representatives of Avito, Raiffeisenbank and Otkritie Bank. We promise a lot of useful information and of course, informal communication. Register for a meeting, invite interested colleagues. Under the cut - theses of speeches, links to the registration (upd: closed) and video broadcast mitap.



Program


Screenshots as a service. Sergey Melyukov (Avito)



I'll tell you about the motivation and difficulties of creating a service for screenshots of web pages based on headless chrome and puppeteer. The availability of ready-made tools does not mean the availability of a ready service.


How to start using AST. Ivan Strelkov (Avito)



In the report, I want to highlight the issue of using AST parsers to automate routine in the frontend. The main purpose of the report is to dispel the view that the use of AST is rocket science.


DX w / Flow. Alexander Lobashev (Raiffeisen)



Let's talk about DX and Flow: how with the help of typing you can increase your productivity and improve the quality of the product.


Javascript processing for dummies. Dmitry Shmakov (Otkritie Bank)


Communication in the form of textual information is the most common method of communication between people and interfaces on the Internet. Solutions that are written in Python or R are usually used to process such information, but developers are not limited to solving applied problems. In my report, I will show how, for example, using native JavaScript, correct errors in words, understand what part of speech they relate to, correct them, or how to classify texts and understand what human language they were written in.
