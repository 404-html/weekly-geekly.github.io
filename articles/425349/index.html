Positive Hack Days 9 Forum will take place on May 21 and 22 at Crocus Expo



Friends, we announce the dates of the ninth forum of Positive Hack Days: it will take place on May 21 and 22, 2019. The next forum will be held at the new venue, the Crocus Expo International Exhibition Center will be the venue.

The number of PHDays guests is growing every year: in 2018 more than five thousand people visited us! The Crocus Expo venue is much larger than the World Trade Center we are used to, so now there will definitely be enough space for an even greater number of participants and guests.
