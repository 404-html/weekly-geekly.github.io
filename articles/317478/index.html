December 16 - the official launch of the Imagine Cup competition in Russia! Come to find out more.

This year our main student competition, the Imagine Cup, has undergone important changes , both internationally and in Russia. To tell more about these changes, we decided to hold a special event - the official launch of the Imagine Cup in Russia .



Where: Microsoft office, Moscow, Krylatskaya str., 17 bld.1
When: December 16, 2016, 15: 00-22: 00
Registration: here

What will happen at the event


  • We will tell you how the official contest rules have changed.
  • We will tell you how the competition will be held this year in Russia.
  • We will give a brief overview of the most modern technologies that will be useful in projects: Bot Framework, IoT, Xamarin, Azure ML, etc.
  • For those who wish - we will conduct two laboratory work on IoT and the creation of bots. To participate in the lab - you need to bring a laptop running Windows 10, with Visual Studio 2015 installed
  • Successful Imagine Cup project managers will share their experience in organizing teams and distributed work on student projects.
  • At the end of the event, we will provide time for individual consultation with experts / mentors and register the already formed projects for a special mentoring / nano-acceleration program .

The full program of the event is available on the website .

Major changes


The main changes in the rules of the contest, which you should know about:

  • The main prize in the international competition increased to $ 100K
  • The size of the team is now from 1 to 3 people. We analyzed the trend of past years and realized that most of the teams fit into 3 people (for example, the winners of 2015 and 2016 from Russia, for example).
  • The main focus this year will be on the technical implementation of the project (50% of the weight in official evaluation criteria). At the same time , the use of Microsoft Azure cloud technologies is mandatory .
  • One category of competition replaces three categories. You no longer need to choose which category to participate in, as well as customize your project under the category

As a result: any modern high-tech project can participate in the Imagine Cup competition (if it uses the Microsoft Azure cloud).
