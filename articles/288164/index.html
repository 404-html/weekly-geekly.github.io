New Banner Advertising Formats

Members of the American Publishers Association (OPA), and this is the largest American media with a cumulative audience of 108 million people, have agreed to try out three new advertising formats for banner advertising.

  • XXL Box (recommended width 468, height 648) - such a multimedia window with tabs, pages and the ability to run video.
