MediaTek Labs invites wearable electronics developers to a free webinar

On December 7, 2016, MediaTek Labs invites you to a free seminar for developers of devices for the Internet of things!



Creating wearable devices is much easier when you have all the necessary tools at hand: HDK, SDK technical documentation. MediaTek Labs proposes to developers to use as a basis for creating wearable devices for the Internet of Things a new LinkIt 2523 HDK breadboard board, controlled by SAC (Smart Application Controller).

Register for the webinar at the link: goo.gl/1SzYWQ

The new breadboard model LinkIt 2523 HDK, controlled by SAC, was created specifically for developers of advanced wearable devices. The board is built on the MediaTek MT2523G chipset, equipped with a dual-mode Bluetooth module, fully supports GNSS standards and has the best performance and energy efficiency among such devices, as well as the speed of the first location and its accuracy. The LinkIt 2523 HDK is almost ideal as a basis for developing wearable devices such as smart watches, fitness bracelets, various monitoring medical devices, etc.

In the first half hour of the webinar, MediaTek Labs technical consultant Philip Hanshin will demonstrate LinkIt 2523 HDK and show you where to start working with the board. The second half of the webinar will traditionally be devoted to questions and answers.

Register for the webinar at the link: goo.gl/1SzYWQ
