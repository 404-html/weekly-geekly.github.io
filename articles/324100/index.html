Welcome to the new Hardaton

Restrike, a subsidiary of Rostelecom, invites everyone to take part in Hardaton (the word is formed from two - Hardware Hackaton).



Hardaton participants have to assemble a working prototype of the device at the junction of electronics, programming and digital production in 48 hours. It is allowed to use any available materials and everything that will go to the prepared site: debugging boards, tools, electronic components, 3D printers, etc.

Engineers, programmers, designers are invited to participate in Hardaton, as well as all those who are fond of electronics, programming, designing devices and want to realize their idea. The topic is not limited - the device should be controlled over the network and do something useful.


Hardaton will be held April 21-23, 2017 at the Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology on the territory of the Skolkovo Innovation Center at ul. Nobel, 3.

For two days of a continuous marathon, all participants will be provided with equipped places for work and a recreation area, spare parts and equipment necessary for the project, good nutrition and strong coffee. Inventors will be advised by experienced engineers.

Participation in Hardaton is free, registration is open on the website: hardathon.ru
