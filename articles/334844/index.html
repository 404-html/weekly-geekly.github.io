Redmine recurring tasks


Do you have regular tasks? Say, hold a meeting on Mondays at 10:30, form a report to the client on Thursdays at 18:00? Or does the customer roll updates on Tuesdays, and the admin should be in constant communication from 10:00? Need to start a working day with a checklist? 20 number to pay the rent?


If you work in Redmine, you can not put a regular task using regular tools. You have to put it manually each time. This is extra time, extra attention and risk of error.


To eliminate the human factor, we have developed a free redmine_recurring_tasks plugin that automatically sets the task daily, weekly, or monthly.


The plugin is compatible with other popular plugins, in particular with redmine_tags and redmine_checklists . It is possible to manually configure the copying of attributes from other plugins. If you have questions about compatibility, check in the comments.


Use on health!
