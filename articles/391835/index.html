Tesla electric cars detained at the entrance to the mountain resort for environmental reasons



Legislation and decrees of most countries related to the transport sector, designed to work with conventional vehicles with internal combustion engines. Electric vehicles, although no longer a novelty, are within the law of such countries the same as cars with internal combustion engines. Of course, this situation is not typical for all states, but the same Singapore has already managed to distinguish itself.

Now a funny situation has arisen in Kazakhstan. Here, however, no one fined anyone, just a group of Tesla electric cars were detained at the entrance to the Medeu high-mountain complex (it is located in the vicinity of Almaty). The traffic police officers of Kazakhstan, who were guided by the decree of President Nursultan Nazarbayev, detained the cars. The decree prohibits entry of vehicles into the complex, for environmental reasons.

One of the band members recorded a video that, in general, is boring, but there is something interesting. For example, on the road past the same traffic policemen, a bus is quietly traveling, which clearly emit more harmful substances into the atmosphere than Tesla (the latter, except that dust on the road is capable of raising).

The traffic police explained that they were aware of what Tesla electric cars are and how they work, but the decree is a decree.
