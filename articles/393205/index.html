Counter-Strike 1.6 on Android is a great way to train to do headshots in the absence of a PC or laptop



Counter-Strike does not lose popularity. This game, in which the forces of evil and good come together, or of terrorists and their opponents from power structures, is still in demand. Versions change, new features are added, the game becomes more beautiful and dynamic from version to version. But what is interesting is that if, among other toys, only new items get players' attention, in the case of Counter-Strike, previous releases remain "afloat".

For example, Counter-Strike 1.6. Gamers, from young to old, do not cease to conduct battles both against each other and against bots. But in order to achieve a certain result (and respect for her associates), you must always be in shape. In Counter-Strike, reflexes and coordination of player's movements (not to mention the team play) play a huge role. In order not to lose shape, you can now even use an Android tablet. Relatively recently, an unofficial version for this OS appeared.

Its creator is programmer Alibek Omarov. To run the toy you need a copy of the original version of Counter-Strike 1.6, plus some additional files and software. Download the APK here .



Worked out all pretty carefully. You can play without additional accessories, using special control points on the screen. But this, of course, is not at all what a lover of "contra" expects. In order to make the game convenient and comfortable, it is desirable to connect a Bluetooth keyboard and mouse. In order to shoot and fall normally, headshading, you need a mouse and keyboard, you can't do without it.

As for the virtual control buttons, they are all customizable, as the creator on Reddit talks about.

Installing a toy on Android is not so easy, so the author offers additional instructions so that there are no difficulties.
