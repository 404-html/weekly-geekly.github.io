“Yandex” and “Channel One” launched live broadcasts of the show “Voice” in the search results

The show “Voice” is searched in “Yandex” more often than the dollar rate. The search engine and Channel One have built in a player to conduct live broadcasts from the output to show-related requests. At the time when “Voice” is not broadcast live, viewers can watch a selection of significant moments from the previous release.



The TV show "Voice" was launched on the "First Channel" in 2012. This is the Russian adaptation of The Voice show, which has been on the Dutch channel RTL4 since 2010. The fifth season of the show started on September 4, 2015.

The popularity of the show led to the fact that during the first four seasons, users searched for him at Yandex a half million times a week on average — what about the dollar rate, except for the periods from November 2014 to January 2015 and July-August 2015 .

Yandex and Channel One for the first time united in the field of live broadcasts. In issuing a search engine viewers can watch the show live or remember the best moments of the last issue.
