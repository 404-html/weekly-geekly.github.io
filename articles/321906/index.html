Broadcast TensorFlow Developer Summit

Friends, very soon, at 20:30 Moscow time, the TensorFlow Developer Summit will begin broadcasting.

Keynout will be led by people like Jeff Dean, Rajat Monga and Megan Kacholia.
I highly recommend watching this broadcast tonight to anyone who is interested in the topic of machine learning. It will be told both about TensorFlow itself and about interesting examples of its use.



Detailed schedule, speakers, etc. can be viewed at the link .

For those who do not know, TensorFlow is a completely open library for machine learning from Google. TensorFlow is used in a huge number of Google products, among them, Search, Gmail, Photos, Youtube, Translate, Assistant, as well as all sorts of recognition algorithms. More information about him can be found on the project website .
