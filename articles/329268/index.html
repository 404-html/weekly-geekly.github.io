Microsoft will open data center in Johannesburg and Cape Town

According to a report in the official blog, the company will have the 40th region of hosting its own data centers. According to Microsoft, this is more than any other cloud provider.


/ Flickr / hans wakataitea / CC

The company has been present in the region for a long time with other small business development programs and increasing the general level of literacy in software development. These initiatives include the news about the launch of its own data center, which will also be part of another more global project, Cloud for Global Good.

Among the main products that will be available in the new region as early as 2018, Microsoft Azure, Office 365 and Dynamics 365 are noted. Their main audience is local startups and public organizations. Major examples include M-KOPA (energy) and AGIN (services for farmers).

One of the main advantages of the local data center is the new opportunities for the financial sector and other types of companies, to which local legislation imposes particularly stringent requirements for working with personal data .
