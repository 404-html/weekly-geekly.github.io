Twice a year, we tell you about the survey. Developer Economics is the world's largest study on global trends in application economics and mobile development. And now the 12th survey for developers, which focuses on the tools for developers, their skills and fees.



Why go through this survey?

Russia is one of the leading mobile markets in the world and we need to back it up with facts and figures. In addition, this survey was compiled by the developers, so all questions will be relevant to you, plus you will learn something new for yourself (for the first time in the survey, elements of gamification are used) - and it will take you only 15 minutes.
