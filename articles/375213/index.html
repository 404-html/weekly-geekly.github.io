The first ad of bitcoins on TV launched



BitPay service, which provides a set of services for retailers who want to accept payment in Bitcoins, launched a television advertising campaign - for the first time in the history of cryptocurrency. Two commercials present the advantages of bitcoins - low commission for payments and increased security when used for online purchases compared to traditional bank cards. This is the site Campaign Live . The author of the commercials is Felton Interactive Group agency.

BitPay is a popular service, its services are used by large companies, which by the end of 2014 announced support for bitcoins. Among them - Dell, Microsoft , PayPal , Newegg. BitPay has long been trying the ground for advertising promotion - in 2014, the company sponsored a game of American football in Florida between college teams. In social networks videos have caused positive feedback.
