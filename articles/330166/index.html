Your brand is at EuroPython 2017

Last fall, I posted here my topic How does the Python parser work, and how to reduce memory consumption by three times . In the spring, I proposed this topic to the organizers of EuroPython 2017, and a week ago I learned that my report had been accepted at the conference.



My current employer is indifferent to Python and Pythonovods, so I will go to the conference either as an individual or as a representative of your company . In return, I expect (in full or in part) to cover the costs of the conference (entrance ticket, flight, accommodation).
