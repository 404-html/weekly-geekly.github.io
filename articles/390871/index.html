Asus Zenbook Pro UX501VW Laptop Video Review



Asus Zenbook Pro UX501VW - productive laptops in a thin and lightweight aluminum package, equipped with a matte 15.6-inch IPS-display with a resolution of 3840x2160 (there are versions where this touch screen, as well as versions with FullHD). Laptops work on Intel Core i7-6700HQ processors, the amount of RAM is up to 24 GB (DDR4, one slot is available, 8 GB are soldered on the board). M.2 NVMe solid state drives are used for data storage up to 512 GB. The GTX 960M graphics card (with 2 or 4 GB of GDDR5 memory) is responsible for the graphics. Laptops are equipped with two types of batteries: 96 Wh (in the review) and 60 Wh. In the latter case, thanks to a smaller battery, an additional 2.5-inch hard drive cage is installed in the laptop. It should be noted that the notebook has a hardware encryption module (TPM), a USB Type-C Gen2 + Thunderbolt 3 port and the Windows 10 Pro operating system.
