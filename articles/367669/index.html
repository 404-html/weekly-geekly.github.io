Apollo Robbins - The Art of Distracting



The greatest pickpocket in the world, Apollo Robbins deftly snatches his wallets, while studying the characteristics of people's behavior. In his cheerful speech, Robbins, using the example of TEDGlobal Hall 2013, shows how selective perception helps pull a wallet and put it on the shoulder of a host who does not even know about it.
