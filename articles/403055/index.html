Sony Xperia XA1 smartphone is available for pre-order

Good news friends! One of the new Sony products that were presented at the MWC 2017 can already be bought in Russia. Rather, you can pre-order a smartphone Sony Xperia XA1.



It will cost exactly 22,990 rubles, and you can pre-order at once in three places: in the company's online store Store.Sony.Ru , as well as in Sony Center and Svyaznoy retail chains.



Recall Xperia XA1 - this is the most affordable smartphone in the new line. It uses a 5-inch IPS-screen with a resolution of 1280x720 pixels with 2.5D rounded glass and is built on the MediaTek Helio P20 platform with an eight-core central processor, in which 4 cores operate at a frequency of 2.3 GHz and 4 others operate at a frequency of 1.6 GHz. The smartphone has 3 GB of RAM and 32 GB of memory permanent, supports work with two SIM-cards. True, the second slot is combined with a slot for a microSD memory card that supports media up to 256 GB.


The side frames are extremely thin, so the smartphone remains very compact for a device with a five-inch screen. In place of the main camera - a 23-megapixel module, used in past Sony flagships, in particular Xperia XZ. Available body colors: gold, pink, white and black.
