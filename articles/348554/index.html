Welcome to Cradle: Rave. And no, this is not a disco

From 16 to 18 February in Skolkovo, we will hold Cradle: Rave. This is the biggest event of Sberbank Technologies, which will bring together everything we do - Artificial Intelligence, Machine learning, chat bots, quantum technologies, DevOps, IoT, blockchain, big data, mobile development. On the last two topics we will organize hackathons with cash prizes, on the blockchain - a jam session in the exotic Pecha-Kucha format, and we will spice it up with a lot of interesting reports and meetings. Details under the cut.



Hackathons of big data and mobile development will take place during all three days, but the teams can not be in the event area all the time. The main thing - to be noted at the opening on Friday evening, to return on Sunday to protect the project and take the money. The site will be supported by data scientists and the team of the competence center for the development of BI technologies Sbertech, as well as team leaders on Android and iOS.
