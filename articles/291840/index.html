HeadHunter calculated how salaries of university graduates change

The Ministry of Education and Science published data on the salaries of graduates in 2013, and HeadHunter read how salaries in different industries change over time after employment.

According to the Ministry of Education, graduates of 2013 who entered employment in 2014 could apply for maximum salary in the field of economics and management. Graduates of the Moscow Higher School of Social and Economic Sciences could count on a salary of 110 thousand rubles. Representatives of the IT industry and lawyers arranged for a salary of 80-100 thousand rubles.



HeadHunter analyzed salary changes. The most profitable professions are lawyers, bank employees and programmers. The figures presented by HeadHunter differ from the information of the Ministry of Education. The highest salary at the beginning of a career awaits programmers, but Moscow companies offer them 44 thousand rubles a month. Lawyers will receive 38 thousand. Employees of sales, bank employees and advertisers can get a salary of 35 thousand rubles.

After three years, programmers receive an average of 86 thousand. Employees of banking, marketing and investment at this time receive 60-69 thousand rubles.

In five years, programmers can count on 120 thousand a month. Lawyers and marketers at this time reach 86 thousand rubles.

Seven or eight years of experience in the popular field can count on 100 or more thousand rubles. Programmers in the position of development directors begin to receive 230 thousand, as well as managers of the banking sector.
