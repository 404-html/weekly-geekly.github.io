Meet Magento Russia 2015: Conference Results



On October 30, the International Meet Magento Russia conference dedicated to one of the world's most popular online store management systems, Magento Commerce, ended in Digital October in Moscow.

The event was organized by our team - ASTRIO.agency

The conference included presentations from the technical and management fields, covering topics such as Magento 2 release, the possibilities of different versions of the platform, secrets of going online to offline, audience analysis in online stores, business secrets in Magento, and maintenance issues of Russian and foreign projects.



Headliner Meet Magento Russia 2015 became Ben Marks - Magento-evangelist from the head office of American Magento Inc. His report was devoted to Magento 2 coming out in November.



Fyodor Virin (Data Insight) and Askar Rakhimberdiev (MoySklad) spoke in detail about the capabilities and tools for analyzing online stores. From year to year such analytics is very popular with guests and participants of the event.



Ilya Kogan (Yandex.Market) caused a stir with an overview of the new products of the service on which many Russian stores place their goods. The audience for a long time did not let the speaker even after questions from the audience.



After lunch there was a series of technical reports. Among the speakers were Andrey Tataranovich (Amasty) with a report on everyday technical support, Alexander Usunov (TagesJump) spoke about the cloud environment for Magento in Amazon WS, and Igor Bondarenko from Neklo spoke on the development process of modules for Magento.



Many thanks to the speakers, sponsors, partners, guests and participants of Meet Magento Russia 2015. See you next year!
