Will the super drones race be super sports?

Nowadays you will not surprise anyone with a miniature flying multi-screw device - or a drone. Drones have already invented enough applications in military, industry, and transport. But for the majority of their owners, they are, above all, a hobby, a hobby. People who are enthusiastic, as you know, love to show off their offspring and be measured by their steepness. All existing technical sports began sometime with a handful of enthusiasts. Now we are watching the emergence of a new species - dronogonok. Under the cut - the latest news from the fields of sports battles and a lot of interesting video.


Actually, in the competitions of drones there is no news either - there are now plenty of them among various categories of devices and according to different rules. The question is, will they remain fun for fans or go to the masses, overgrown with fans and budgets? Or, in another way, will Formula 1 drones ever appear? Of course, we are not able to answer this question precisely, but there is some interesting movement about which we would like to tell.



On February 4, the first class X drone races took place in California. This class includes devices with a frame length of 1 meter between engines (plus or minus 15 cm). All other characteristics are not regulated. Why was such a large size chosen? The answer here is obvious: for more entertainment. Large drone can be seen from afar, and it falls beautifully (where without it). There are other reasons.
