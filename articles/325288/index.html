GoTech 2017 technological projects competition has started

Applications for participation in the 9th GoTech technology projects competition are open . This year, the main competitive nomination received the name GoGlobal. Along with it, at least 10 industry tracks are planned, the first of which, Digital Experience, today announced Beeline.

GoTech nominations winners will be able to launch joint pilots with corporations, speak at the world's leading start-up forums, qualify for investments, grants and other prizes.



100 experts of venture funds, corporations, business incubators and accelerators will take part in the selection of the finalists of the independent GoGlobal nomination . The main evaluation criterion: can the project take over the world and become a global success story?

The first industry-wide Digital Experience nomination was established by Beeline. Companies and development teams are invited to offer users a new experience in using mobile devices: AR / VR without a helmet, geolocation, mobile payments, face and object recognition, loyalty programs, gamification, social networking solutions.

You can find out the details of the first nominations and submit an application on the website or on the contest page on Facebook , Vk .
