MS Office for Android tablets - freely available



Two months ago, Microsoft began a closed beta test of three MS Office applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) for Android tablets. Invites were distributed through the community in Google Plus .

Now the testing program has been expanded , and free applications can be downloaded by anyone from the Google Play catalog, without recording to the queue and waiting.

Word for Android tablets
Excel for Android tablets
PowerPoint for Android tablets

It should be noted that these are specialized versions of applications specifically for tablets (the same ones were released a little earlier for the iPad), while options for smartphones appeared a year and a half earlier, but they cannot be installed on tablets. These versions differ in interface and functionality.









Initially, mobile versions of MS Office were paid. To create and edit documents, paid Office 365 accounts were required. Last year, all the basic functions of MS Office became free. All you need is a regular Microsoft account. Well, meet the system requirements: Android 4.4+, the screen from 7 to 10.1 inches.

Probably, if a couple of years ago someone had said that Microsoft would provide free access to its main commercial product - an office application package, it would hardly be believed in such words. Nevertheless, the freemium model still provides for the possibility of income.
