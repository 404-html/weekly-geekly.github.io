The Ministry of Communications of the Russian Federation has published key figures and facts for 2014

The press release on the ministry's website contains key facts and figures from the field of information technology, telecommunications, media, e-government and mail. About new workplaces in technology parks, places for students and about 4G in Russia - under the cut.



In 2014, more than 400,000 square meters with 18 thousand jobs were built in 10 technology parks. A separate entry was made to the final stage of construction of the Innopolis innovation center, which houses the first specialized university in Russia in the field of IT and robotics. From 2014, universities will take on 35% more students for IT specialties at the expense of the budget allocations of the federal budget.

Technological neutrality for 4G began to operate in the most popular frequency ranges 450 MHz, 900 MHz and 1800 MHz. Earned a law on the transition to another operator with his old number, more than 920 thousand numbers have already been transferred. Fiber-optic communication lines will appear in 14 thousand settlements with the number of inhabitants from 250 to 500 people - the order was signed by Vladimir Putin last year. LTE networks have appeared in 36 regions of Russia.

Spacecraft Express-AM5, Express-AT1, Express-AT2 and Express-AM6, intended for communication, were launched and put into operation. Let me remind you that in 2014 Russia carried out 38 space launches and put 80 spacecraft into orbit .

Work continues in the field of e-government. 4355 service centers now confirm the identity of users; for the period from August to December 2014, 277,000 users of public services underwent this procedure. The Tula and Ivanovo regions have signed agreements on participation in the GIS housing and communal services system, the information and referral system of the housing and utilities sector. The system, which is integrated into the portal of state services, will allow to obtain reliable information about utility tariffs.
