About a third of mobile advertising in the Mail.ru Group system is bought by the Chinese


Image: Roem

Today, Mail.ru Group has published statistics on the use of the myTarget platform. As it turned out, foreign companies buy most of the advertising. They account for more than 60% of mobile advertising in myTarget, while Russian companies and individuals, respectively, account for 40%.

Among foreigners, Chinese companies buy the most advertising, they account for 37% of all mobile advertising in myTarget. 20% is accounted for by European companies, 4% - by the USA.

myTarget is a unified advertising platform, which includes such platforms as Odnoklassniki, Vkontakte, My World. The total coverage of myTarget is about 140 million.

MyTarget has powerful targeting tools. So, advertisers have the opportunity to choose the target audience, with an indication of demographic, behavioral and other criteria. According to the developers, the system also has a transparent pricing system.
