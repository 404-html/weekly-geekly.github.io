RamQA # 06 - November 29 at 19:00



November 29 at 19:00 we will hold the next meetup, dedicated to software testing.

This time our guest will perform:

Yana Valishevskaya, Rambler Digital Solutions
"Who is the Test Analyst and how is it useful?"
What is a test analyst? What is different from the tester? How to become a test analyst? What is the benefit for the project? The purpose of this presentation is to provide answers to frequently asked questions and introduce some subtleties of work.


Alexander Romanov, Deutsche Bank RTC.
"The episode where Cucumber meets Scala."
In the report, I will talk about whether to write BDD tests with Cucumber, how to do it with Scala and how to quickly prepare a project for writing tests.


Egor Kotov and Nikolay Burmistrov, Rambler & Co
"Strategic testing and quality management."
We will talk about achieving business goals through testing, quality management, increasing functionality and prioritizing testing goals. And also talk a little about the evaluation of the effectiveness of testing.
