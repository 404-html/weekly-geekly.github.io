How professionals work. Evgenia Arestova, General Director of the Omsk Data Center



The heading "How Professionals Work" continues to acquaint the readers of "Megamind" with the working habits of various specialists in the IT market. On the eve of International Women's Day, we decided to introduce to our audience another interesting female specialist working in an important position.

Evgenia Arestova is the head of the Omsk data center under construction (Tier III), one of the largest in the Urals, around which it is planned to erect a small town with its own logistics park, agricultural complex and low-rise buildings for 14 thousand people. Also, the general director of the company "Open Business Space", created to ensure the full cycle of construction and project management "Data Center" Omsk ".

The biography of our heroine is more than representative: there are two higher educations (Omsk Pedagogical University and Moscow Academy of Finance and Industry), work in the field of consulting and public administration in the city administration. Since 2011, Evgenia held a position in the Department of Urban Economic Policy, from where she returned to consulting in 2014, this time in the construction field. Raises five children.
