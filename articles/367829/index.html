Microsoft began to distribute Windows 10 as a recommended update.

This method can help increase the market share of Windows 10.



Back in November last year, it became known that the Windows 10 operating system will be distributed as a recommended update. This method, according to representatives of Microsoft, can help increase the share of the new OS on computers. Now the company has begun to fulfill the promise, and from the 1st the recommended update appeared on the PC of many users of previous versions of Windows.

"As we promised, we are now ready to ease the transition to Windows 10 for users of Windows 7 and Windows 8.1," a spokesman said.

Since this is an optional update, Windows users may opt out. And users can roll back the update within 31 days. Previously, Microsft began automatically loading Windows 10 on users' computers in order to speed up the upgrade process. Last month, according to Microsoft, the number of Windows 10 users was already 200 million people. Windows 10 market share was over 10%, according to information from Net Applications.



In this case, the most active segment of users of the new OS are gamers. The fact is that Windows 10 promises performance gains in games through the use of Direct X. Steam statistics show that every fourth gamer has this OS installed.

Microsoft is planning to install Windows 10 on a billion devices in 2-3 years. This is quite possible, since the system is universal - it works on PCs, smartphones with tablets, and Xbox gaming consoles.
