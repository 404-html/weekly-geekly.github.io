On the day of the Fallout 4 release, pornhub traffic fell by 10%



According to the vice president of PornHub Corey Price, on the day of the release of the game Fallout 4, attendance at one of the largest porn hubs decreased by 10%. "I can not say that we were very surprised by the fact that our traffic at the time of the release of this game fell," he added. The decline in activity on Pornhub was observed from night to three o'clock the next day.
