GeekUniversity opens faculty of iOS development


As you know, the educational portal GeekBrains, together with the Mail.Ru Group, recently launched Russia's first online university for programmers GeekUniversity . By that time, GeekBrains had been working for 7 years in the field of online education, accumulated 130,000 student reviews and assessments, gathered a million-strong community of beginners and experienced programmers, marketers, and web designers, concluded partnerships with major IT companies in Russia, and trained tens of thousands of specialists in the whole world.


Great news - the faculty of iOS development opens at GeekUniversity!


The educational program GeekUniversity was created using techniques that are used in the best educational centers in the United States:


  • project-oriented learning;
  • personal tutor;
  • electives from large IT projects;
  • support at all stages of training;
  • job security.

Flows in the faculties of web development and Android development start every month. And now the recruitment of students to the new department of iOS development is announced!



At the faculty, they master the skills of iOS development at the Middle-level, and there is an opportunity to start a career while studying. For 1 year, students will create 4 services, pump over Soft Skills, which they will check at interviews, work in a team of professional developers, and learn programming techniques from leading industry experts.
