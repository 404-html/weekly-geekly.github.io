Video LIFE.FILM received funding

As written by the guys in the blog "LIFE.FILM" - converts any video into bright musical films about your life.
So far, the project has attracted only a portion of the amount of $ 20 thousand in exchange for a 10 percent stake from business angel Alexei Karlov.

LIFE.FILM can unite people into a network of "live" video - the so-called mosaic, component, part of general stories. LIFE.FILM - a time machine for the memories. Artificial Intelligence itself chooses the best moments from the original video. When searching, attention is paid to persons, images, movement, frame color coverage, etc.

LIFE.FILM can unite people into a global network of live video mosaic. Part of life becomes part of shared stories.

According to firrma , based on the words of the co-founder of the service Rustam Salavatov, the investor received 10% in the company, and $ 20,000 is only the first tranche of investments, and the total amount of investments by Alexei Karlov has not yet been determined. It also became known that no one has ever evaluated the startup itself.
