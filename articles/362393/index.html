Ready to give money to huge battle robots?

MegaBots, Inc. He is now trying to raise $ 1.8 million on Kickstarter to make his dream come true - to create huge battle robots. Such as were in the movie "Living Steel" or in the last "Matrix". They will be called Megabots.

By the way, for only $ 999,999, you can pick up one such robot for yourself.

One of the creators:
This is amazing: huge robots step into the arena and destroy each other!




These mad scientists are working on a humanoid robot 4.5 meters high and weighing almost seven tons. Replaceable air guns - instead of hands. Each robot will be controlled by a team of shooter and pilot.

The hulls of Megabot will be covered with armor from special plates that break when hit with a projectile filled with paint, flying at a speed of 120 miles per hour. The computer will calculate the amount of damage to the limbs and torso of the robot. The more hits - the more the robot will limp, the weapon will jam. After causing a sufficient amount of damage to a certain part of the robot - it will turn off.

The company plans to build more than one robot: the more money they collect, the more they build. Goal: run the first championship among combat robots.
