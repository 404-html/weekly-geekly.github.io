Who is most written online media

What kind of people and organizations writes the specific online edition most of all - unique data for Runet. This information is provided in the new reports of the Inforotor Statistics System.

The reports indicate the number of news with a reference to a particular person (organization), as well as the number of visits to this news on the online media site. Thus, the report shows about whom the Internet edition most often writes and about whom the audience of this media most reads.



Clicking on the number of news or visits, you can see in which news this person or organization was mentioned.

We believe that these reports will be a useful tool for establishing more effective cooperation between Internet publications and the press services of the organizations and persons mentioned in the news.
