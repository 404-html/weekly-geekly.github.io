Top Searches for Google in 2014



Today, Google has published two lists: the most popular search queries in 2014. However, one list applies only to the United States, and the other to the whole world.

In principle, both lists are similar, but there are some minor differences.

Requests in the USA


  • Robin williams
  • World Cup
  • Ebola
  • Malaysia Airlines
  • Flappy bird
  • ALS Ice Bucket Challenge
  • ISIS
  • Ferguson
  • Frozen
  • Ukraine


Worldwide


  • Robin williams
  • World Cup
  • Ebola
  • Malaysia Airlines
  • ALS Ice Bucket Challenge
  • Flappy bird
  • Conchita wurst
  • ISIS
  • Frozen
  • Sochi Olympics


Interestingly, for the first time in several years, Apple products have not been added to any of the lists. Prior to this, starting in 2010, certain places in the top 10 were occupied by search queries related to iPad, iPhone 5, iPad 3, iPhone 5S.
