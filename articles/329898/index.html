Must see: videos of MoscowJS 37 mitap



On Thursday, May 25, the next meeting of the MoscowJS front-end developer community took place at Avito's office. We discussed the debugging of animation, the creation of WebGL visualization, webpack assembly and code review. Today we publish video recordings of reports - it will be interesting for professionals. Enjoy watching!

The meeting was opened by Alexander Chernikov (Sberbank Technologies), who told about how their project, starting with jQuery and React (es5), has grown to TypeScript and Node.js: out of memory.



Ivan Strelkov from Avito shared his experience with the code review. How to understand the big pull request and not go crazy, what tools and approaches you can use to do this - see his report.



Developer Tutu.ru Dmitry Shuranov in the smallest details considered the reasons for which there are missing frames in the animation, what it leads to and how to avoid it.



In the closing report, Alexander Amosov told how to implement an interactive 3D map with his own hands, ranging from creating a model in a three-dimensional editor and ending with optimizations from the world of computer games.



According to a good tradition, the mitap ended in a talk in Bartschik (and this time almost without laptops).



Thanks to everyone who came to the mitap and watched the live broadcast! You can follow the announcements of the next MoscowJS meetings on the community site . Photos from the event are already in our group in the FB , and a playlist with all the reports can be found on our channel .

See you again!
