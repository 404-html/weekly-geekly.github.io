Get an investment of up to $ 100,000 and launch joint projects with the leaders of the advertising industry

The association of communication agencies ADV, which includes more than 40 companies, co-organized the first contest of technological projects in the field of media, marketing and advertising Go To AdTech .

Selected companies will have the opportunity to launch pilot projects with agencies involved in ADV, in the interests of large clients. The holders of the first three places will receive grants in the amount of 250 thousand, 150 thousand and 100 thousand rubles, respectively.


The Go To AdTech contest was launched in November 2016 in conjunction with the Untitled ventures foundation, which will offer the winner an investment of $ 100,000. More than 100 projects have already become participants, but due to the advent of the corporate partner, the organizers decided to extend the acceptance of applications until January 31, 2017 The competition operator is GoTech Foundation.
