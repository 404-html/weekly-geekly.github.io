City information systems are integrated into projects of Rambler & Co

According to Gazety.ru, Moscow Mayor Sergey Sobyanin and Chairman of the Board of Directors of Rambler Internet Holding LLC Alexander Mamut signed a cooperation agreement.

According to the document, Rambler & Co services will get access to Internet services for Muscovites, giving additional opportunities for finding housing, car selection, managing apartment and financial tasks.

Special blocks will appear on the resources of Rambler & Co, allowing users to interact with the electronic services of the Moscow government. It is also planned to inform target groups about the conduct of electronic referendums and an increase in the number of the latter.

Rambler & Co is one of the largest Russian companies, uniting various media, technological and e-commerce projects with a total audience of over 40 million people per month.
