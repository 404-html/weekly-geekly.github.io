Coub Creator Criticizes Russian Industry Media

Founder of Coub , co-founder of Look At Me and former head of the Nimbler design studio, Anton Gladkoborodov, published a critical message to industry media on his personal Facebook page.
I do not complain, we are certainly not deprived of attention. About us regularly write, I gave a bunch of interviews over the past two years. Everybody wrote about us anyway. But I am at a loss. I do not understand how you can get past the big news, especially in the press, which is about "technology".
In particular, Anton Gladkoborodov mentions projects with Russian founders, news about which, in his opinion, appear depressingly rarely on the pages of leading online media that are written in Russian.
And you might think that you need projects. But no, you are the one who makes it all bad, and the projects are fine. All of them are fine. This is Kuyda in Y-Combinator, this is King of Thieves 2M in the first 5 days, this Stavitsky sold Bubl. And you need it, because only by creating your stars, spinning projects from your audience, you will be strong. But you are waiting for when the Russian project will shoot in the west or sell Google. But in this case, you will reprint this news from Techcranch.
