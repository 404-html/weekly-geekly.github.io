The revenue of Skolkovo projects is growing faster than the plan.


D. Abramov / Vedomosti

In 2014, Skolkovo projects received revenue in the amount of 27.8 billion rubles, despite the fact that the planned level of revenue was 2 billion rubles. This information is contained in the annual report of Skolkovo submitted to Dmitry Medvedev at the board of trustees of the foundation.

This level of income was received by small innovative companies, Vedomosti reports. At the same time, in Skolkovo itself, no one expected such a rapid growth in start-up revenues.

According to the plan, the revenue of projects from 2010 should have been about 5 billion rubles, but the participants of the fund received much more - in the amount of about 43.6 billion rubles.

Now the number of fund projects has grown to 1070. 45% of all projects received revenue, while the income of 3% exceeded 100 million rubles. In addition to the revenue of these projects, in 2014, Skolkovo managed to over-fulfill the plan for patent applications, having received about 645 applications with a plan of 200. In addition, the plan to raise funds from Skolkovo was practically fulfilled, receiving 4.45 billion rubles. with a plan of 4.5 billion rubles.

Last year, the fund approved grants of about 1.5 billion rubles, while most of these projects fall within the scope of energy efficient technologies. Least of all turned out projects related to the field of IT-technologies.
