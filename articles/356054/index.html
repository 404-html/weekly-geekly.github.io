But on the Internet from the point of view of regulation, everything is much more complicated: instead of one blocked resource, like three hydra heads, three new ones appear. I note that in the Russian Federation, they have been actively fighting both offline and Internet one-armed gangsters and other rogues for a long time. Nevertheless, with regard to the clarity of the regulation of gambling, here Russia is almost ahead of the rest. The English-language resource gamblingsites.com calls our laws "absolutely understandable." There are gaming zones, there are a couple of legal online services, everything else is prohibited. In the United States, for example, everything is much more complicated : the act, adopted in 2006, did not prohibit the activities of online casinos and betting shops, but imposed responsibility on the financial institutions that served them. At present, everything has become even more complicated due to the different regulations in each state. Everything is very difficult and in most European countries with the exception of Britain, where the gaming business is allowed, but is required to receive special licenses and generally be fairly transparent.

Since 2009, the activity of casinos and slot machines outside four specially designated gambling zones has been officially banned in Russia. Gambling sites on the Internet, which are located on foreign domains, also fell under the law. Until 2014, blocking such resources by a court decision was ineffective and only worked for regional providers.

In 2013, the first check of the Prosecutor's Office of the Russian Federation occurred, revealing 219 illegal resources. Based on these materials, the court decided to block sites. The first wave of locks occurred in March 2014.

In the summer of the same year, the Federal Tax Service received extraordinary judicial blocking of casinos, which actively began to use them last year.

PS If you are still unable to cope with casino addiction, be careful and place bets only on those online casinos that have a special license from the Federal Tax Service. These casinos impose a number of stringent requirements in terms of the authorized capital, bank guarantee and assets. Also, the bookmaker, after receiving the license, is obliged to join a special self-regulatory organization (SRO), to which certain requirements are made.

After receiving the license and joining the SRO, the casino can even start working offline. For this, the SRO, in which the casino is located, must create a banking or non-banking credit organization or enter into a share of the already established one. The casino concludes an agreement with this organization on the implementation of the functions of the online betting transfer accounting center (TSUPIS), opens an account there and only after that can it start accepting bets, but only through this system.

There are no other legal options for betting on the Internet.
