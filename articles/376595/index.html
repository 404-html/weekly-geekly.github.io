SkyProwler vertical take-off multirotor

Is it a quadrocopter? This is a plane? This and that, and another! SkyProwler - an aircraft capable of taking off both vertically and with a takeoff run. The device has the function of voice control, removes the chassis in the air and is equipped with a stabilization system for the GoPro camera. February 18, 2015 developers will launch a campaign on Kickstarter.



The SkyProwler aircraft has four screws that can be put inside the case - in this case it flies like a plane with the help of the remaining two screws. Takeoff is carried out vertically or after the run. Wings and tail can be removed or replaced. A stabilization system is built into the nose of the case, which will keep the camera pointing to the desired point, as in that ad about a chicken .

Software and hardware allow SkyProwler to follow the host and fly autonomously along given points.



But the developers are pursuing another goal: to create SkyCruiser - a five-seat hybrid of an airplane with a vertical take-off and a car. The purpose of the device is to save time and money on trips to the airport and flights, allowing the owner of a flying car to get from point A to point B without intermediaries. Unless, of course, legislation will allow.
