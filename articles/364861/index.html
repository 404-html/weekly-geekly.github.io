How is Peter Thiel going to live to 120 years


Peter Thiel is a well-known investor in IT projects (co-founder of PayPal, early investments in Facebook), whose brilliant lecture notes on Startup published on Habr√© - in an interview with Bloomberg Television, he shared with the audience that he was going to live to 120 years and told how it is precisely planning to achieve this.

First of all, 47-year-old Peter daily takes growth hormone somatotropin (one of the hormones of the anterior pituitary gland), which allows him to preserve the amount of muscle mass, thus making less likely bone injuries and the development of arthritis. In addition, he refused to consume sugar, regularly drinks red wine, does not forget about regular jogging and adheres to the Paleo-diet , which provides for the rejection of "modern" food, for example, wheat.

Other life extension efforts that Peter is making are investments in biotech companies. For example, his areas of interest are the use of stem cells for cancer therapy and the cultivation of useful products in the laboratory. It is planned that success in the treatment of cancer will be achieved in the next decade.

However, even this is not enough for Peter - we need dreams. He invested $ 1.25 million in a company planning to build an entire colony-city for free people with libertarian views off the coast of San Francisco on a special platform. Residents of the city will have to enjoy life and be free from the shackles of state laws and moral conventions.
