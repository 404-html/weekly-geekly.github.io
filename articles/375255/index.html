Bitcoin rate dropped significantly at the beginning of the year.

The year 2015 started for Bitcoin is not very good - the currency suddenly fell below $ 300 and continues to fall. Together with the cost, the number of transactions falls (according to the blockchain information).

It is unclear what caused such a sudden fall, because no unpleasant events were recorded. The fall in the value of BTC in the short term is not so terrible - it can be unpleasant except for mining farms. It is likely that out of the system there is an outflow of funds due to the desire of speculators to play in the markets of other currencies.
