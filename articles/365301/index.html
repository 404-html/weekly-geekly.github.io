Video review laptop Gigabyte P35X v3



Gigabyte P35X v3 is a gaming laptop with a display diagonal of 15.6 inches (matte, IPS) and a resolution of 2880x1620. High performance is provided by an Intel Core i7-4710HQ processor and a GeForce GTX 980M video card (8 GB GDDR5). The amount of RAM is 16 GB (two slots available). In the laptop there are two mSATA slots in which solid-state drives are located, combined in raid0 (in the model from the review 128 + 128 GB). In addition to these, a 1 TB hard drive is installed with a rotation speed of 7200 rpm. Separately, it should be noted the possibility of replacing the optical drive on the container for 2.5-inch drives.
