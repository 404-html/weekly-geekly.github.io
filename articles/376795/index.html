Ingress fans delayed the Egyptian police



In Alexandria, Egypt, a group of game lovers Ingress was arrested by local police. The reason for the detention was a gaming event, which brought together 14 players. The police took the meeting for an unauthorized rally. After the players were released from the polling station, the event was canceled.

The alertness of the Egyptian police can be understood - the recent coups and changes of power are still fresh in the memory of the residents. Ingress supporters prefer not to receive permission from the authorities to hold events, as they usually take place in public places, near monuments or other places of interest.

This event is not the first encounter of Ingress lovers. In Melbourne, at one of the meetings, the local police asked the players to disperse. Especially zealous fans of the game from time to time get into places closed to the public.

Ingress is an online multiplayer game for smartphones linked to the real world. For the game you need to move around the area and visit the places marked on the map.
