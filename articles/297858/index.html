From Ridewithme to Withme



Happy New Year, dear readers! We are glad to inform you the good news! The Ridewithme project is even better! We are releasing an update of our application as well as a version for Android devices! Now we will be even more! We changed a lot in our project including icon and name! Meet - WITHME!




Working on the update, we took into account the wishes of all our users. We wanted to make our product better. It took a lot of time and effort to re-invent the application.
Let's start by filtering the event list. Now the filter is simple and convenient. It still has a choice of the type of classes, as well as filtering events by distance from the user.



Using this filter, it is most convenient to monitor what is happening, and a person will always be able to find the nearest interesting activity after work or study.

NO CITIES! Our next idea is to remove cities from the application. All our cities were tied to the IATA parameter. In our database, by default, only those cities that have an airport are displayed. To put it mildly, not all cities have an airport. This led to the fact that we needed to add cities manually at the request of our users. Since the number of cities without air routes is simply huge, it was decided to correct the problem in a fundamental way. We completely removed the city from our application. Our solution looks better than its predecessor by about 143 percent.



To create an event in your unique place, now you just need to throw a pin on the map where it is needed, that's all! This enhancement adds additional user experience when creating an event. If the user does not know where his place of interest is, then in the search box you can enter the name or address of the place.
